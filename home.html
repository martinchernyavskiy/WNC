Connecting minds, advancing tech

Wisconsin Neuromorphic Computing (WNC) champions innovation in neuromorphic technologies. Based in Lisle, we unite students and professionals, offering invaluable resources and events that enhance understanding and collaboration. Our vibrant community fosters the growth of neuromorphic computing, empowering individuals to push the boundaries of technology and creativity. Join us in exploring the future of computing and making impactful contributions in this exciting field.
